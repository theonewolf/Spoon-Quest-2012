Willkommen auf der 2012 Spoon Quest!

Hello, and welcome to this year's version of the Spoon Quest. To get your ultimate Christmas present this year, you must finish the Spoon Quest. You will face a series of increasingly challenging puzzles, three in total this year. This is the first year of Spoon Quest; thus, no official rules exist yet. Spoon Quest was inspired by these brothers and the annual Dropquest. Each challenge requires you to:

  1. Learn a new skill
  2. Think deeply
  3. Compute (human or machine)
